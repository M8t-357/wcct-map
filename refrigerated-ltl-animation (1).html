Step 5 of 7
Point-to-point — loaded once, never touched again
Your freight is loaded once at origin and stays in the same temperature-controlled trailer until it reaches your destination. West Coast Carriers does not transload. We do not move your product between trucks or stage it through a warehouse. Zero transfer risk. Zero temperature breaks.
  • ✓Loaded once at origin — never moved until final delivery
  • ✓No warehouse stops or intermediate transfers
  • ✓Continuous cold chain maintained throughout transit
  • ✓Fewer handoffs means fewer damage and spoilage risks
