Step 3 of 7
Your freight is picked up at origin
Our carrier arrives at your facility with a food-grade, FSMA-compliant refrigerated trailer. Your freight is loaded into a dedicated temperature-controlled compartment — separated from other loads by insulated bulkheads secured to the E-Track system.
  • ✓Food-grade trailer — clean and inspected before loading
  • ✓Insulated bulkheads create your dedicated compartment
  • ✓Temperature set and verified before departure
  • ✓Bill of lading and temperature documentation completed

Step 5 of 7
Point-to-point — no transloading, ever
Your freight is loaded once and stays in the same compartment until it reaches your destination. West Coast Carriers does not transload — we do not move your product from one trailer to another or stage it through a warehouse. Zero transfer risk. Zero temperature breaks.
  • ✓Loaded once at origin — never touched again until delivery
  • ✓No warehouse stops or intermediate transfers
  • ✓Continuous cold chain maintained throughout transit
  • ✓Fewer handoffs means fewer damage and spoilage risks
